What Reticle is

Reticle is a personal journaling and self-assessment tool: you record predictions with a confidence level, resolve them yourself, and the app computes statistics about your own judgment. Reticle makes no predictions of its own, offers no advice, and involves no wagering, prizes, or real-money outcomes of any kind. Your scores reflect only the entries you chose to log and how you chose to resolve them.
