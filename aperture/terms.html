1. What Aperture is — and isn’t

Aperture is a practice and attention-discipline tool for the Controlled Remote Viewing (CRV) protocol. It presents randomized hidden targets, records your impressions, and scores trait overlap after reveal.

The app makes no claims of psychic ability, paranormal function, or predictive power. Scores measure vocabulary overlap between your entries and a target’s tagged traits — nothing more. The app is provided for personal practice, curiosity, and entertainment. It is not a medical, psychological, diagnostic, financial, or decision-making tool, and it must not be used as one.
