Quiet Optics

Instruments for the mind.

Precise, private iPhone tools for measuring and training how you think — one careful app at a time.

Quiet Optics is a small studio building precise, private instruments for the questions people usually only get sold answers to. Remote viewing, forecasting, intention, precognition — the corners of experience where the market runs on confident readings and monthly belief. We don't sell the answer. We build the instrument, hand it to you, and let your own record do the talking.

Every app here does the same disciplined thing: you make a call, seal it before you know the outcome, resolve it later, and get scored honestly against a fair baseline. If the effect is real for you, the statistics will show it. If it isn't, they'll show that too — plainly, without a wince or a pep talk. That two-sidedness is not a caveat we bury; it is the whole point. An instrument you can talk into agreeing with you isn't an instrument.

We take no position on whether any of this is real. Neither does the app — that's exactly why it's worth measuring, carefully, for you. Maybe it's noise. Maybe it isn't. This is how you'd actually find out. Skeptic-friendly, believer-respecting, sober enough to trust and curious enough to bother.

About

Quiet Optics is a small studio building focused tools for attention, judgment, and self-knowledge. Every app runs on your device — no accounts, no analytics, no servers. What you record stays yours.

Instruments, not oracles. Where these apps use on-device AI at all, it plays referee, not fortune-teller — it checks the call you already made against what actually happened, on your iPhone, and it never once tells you what's going to happen next.
